Welcome to the Strategic Decision-Making Experiment.


Overview: Pre-Experiment Questionnaire, Game 1, Game 2, Game 3, Post-Experiment Questionnaire

Players: You, other humans, machine players.

Two types of machine players:
  • a) simple algorithms executing a non-conditional strategy
  • b) sophisticated artificial intelligence agents trained against human players and other artificial players.
You will be asked which type of machine you played with.

Confidentiality: Your answers will be confidential. The researcher has no way to personally identify you.

Performance: Compete and make decisions to the best of your ability, there is a side competition between SOS, ACSC, AWC, and SAASS.

Good luck!
