Thank you for your participation in this experiment.


As you may have noticed in your informed consent document, this game included some level of deception. To clear up any deception, the following experiences during your game were as follows:


Any player listed as human in the game was actually a random number generator.


Any player listed as Artificial Intelligence (AI) was actually a relatively simple algorithm called Tit-for-Tat (TFT), not artificial intelligence. TFT is one of the most effective strategies in the Prisoners Dilemma. In the game of Chicken, the algorithm played a similar strategy. There ARE artificially intelligent players that exist and are very effective. In fact, they exhibit some very interesting, and very non-human, characteristics in simple games such as the ones you played today. If you are interested, the researcher will be happy to discuss the topic with you. Please let him know


Please do not share your experience in the game with anyone until told by the researcher that is ok to discuss. You can understand how a participant's prior knowledge of the information on this page would corrupt the experiment.
