Thank you for your participation in this experiment.


As stated in the introduction, here’s a debrief on your competitors and advisors:

  • All players who were not you played non-conditional strategies in order to control for variation which might have affected your decision-making in ways the experiment could not account for.
  • The human player was a real person.
  • The AI’s (both advisors and competitors) were algorithms which used non-conditional strategies.

Today, in real life, there ARE real artificial intelligence algorithms which can play such games very well and can advise decision-makers. In fact, they exhibit some very interesting and advanced, but very non-human, characteristics. If you are interested in learning more, the researcher will happily discuss the topic with you.


Please do not share your experience in the game with anyone until told by the researcher that is ok to discuss. You can understand how a participant's prior knowledge of the information on this page would corrupt the experiment.
