Scoring: (in each pairing of you and one other player)
  • If you both select peace, you enjoy prosperity and get {{ Constants.both_Peace_payoff }}.


    Peace     +
    Peace     = {{ Constants.both_Peace_payoff }} each.

  • If one of you chooses Peace and the other chooses War, the player who chose Peace gets defeated and gets {{ Constants.betrayed_payoff }} and the player who chose War wins the spoils and gets {{ Constants.betray_payoff }}.


    Peace     +
    War     = Winner: {{ Constants.betray_payoff }}, Loser: {{ Constants.betrayed_payoff}}

    War     +
    War     = {{ Constants.both_War_payoff }} each.
