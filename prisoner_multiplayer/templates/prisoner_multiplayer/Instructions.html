  • An AI decision-maker:

Decisions: In each round, promote or wage with each of these competitors.
Note: You will make three decisions each round (one for each competitor).


Scoring:
  • If you both promote Peace, you both enjoy prosperity and get {{ Constants.both_Peace_payoff }}.


    Peace     +
    Peace     = {{ Constants.both_Peace_payoff }} each.

  • If one of you promotes Peace and the other wages War, the Peace promoter gets defeated (gets {{ Constants.betrayed_payoff }}) and the War wager wins the spoils (gets {{ Constants.betray_payoff }}).


    Peace     +
    War     = War wager: {{ Constants.betray_payoff }}, Peace promoter: {{ Constants.betrayed_payoff}}

  • If both of you wage War, you go to a costly war and both of you receive {{ Constants.both_War_payoff }}.


    War     +
    War     = {{ Constants.both_War_payoff }} each.

    • Therefore, your best interest is based upon the combination of your choice and your adversary's choice. You will not know your adversary's choice until after you select yours, but you can see previous choices.
Goal: Maximize your score!
